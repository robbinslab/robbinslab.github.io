Robbins Visual Cognition Lab

Webcam Eyetracking Study | Robbins Lab

The purpose of this study is to develop a machine learning algorithm that will turn a webcam into an eye tracker. You will conduct simple computer tasks where you will complete trials of visual search. During each trial, you will
search for a particular object (such as a dot or picture of an animal)
on a computer screen. While you search, your webcam will record your
face.

Learn More about Robbins Lab

Compensation: $5 Amazon Gift Card
Time to Complete: ~15 minutes
Preparation: You must be in a distraction-free environment with your computer in a stationary location (e.g. your desk). No other faces can be present on the screen.
Eligibility Requirements: Stationary computers with webcams (e.g., laptop or desktop); No mobile devices or tablets are allowed.
